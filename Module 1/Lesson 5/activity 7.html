Facts about dogs

Dogs are very innocent and loyal animals. They are also very smart. Dogs can be guards for houses and dogs can also work for the police force. There are many type of dog breeds. Everyones most favourite type of dog breed is the golden retriever. Dogs have sharp memories. When someone feeds them, they remember that person for the rest of their life or for a long time. Dogs are overall lovely animals.
